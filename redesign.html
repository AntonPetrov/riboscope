About Riboscope

Founded in 2022, Riboscope provides specialist consultancy in RNA bioinformatics and software development.

We combine expertise in RNA biology, computational pipelines, and interface design to help research teams, biotech startups, and established organisations make the most of their data.

Leadership

Riboscope is led by Anton Petrov, PhD, a bioinformatician with 15+ years of experience in genome annotation, RNA structure analysis, and software development. Anton previously led the Rfam and RNAcentral databases at EMBL-EBI and created R2DT.

Our Approach

Our work spans every stage of the research pipeline — from processing raw data to developing demo-ready web applications. We deliver:

  • Robust RNA analysis pipelines
  • Intuitive search and visualisation tools
  • Interactive web applications and interfaces

We combine scientific depth with a developer's mindset, helping clients turn complex data into actionable insights and working solutions.
