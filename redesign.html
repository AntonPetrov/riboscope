About Riboscope

Founded in 2022, Riboscope is a specialist consultancy in RNA bioinformatics and scientific software development.

With deep expertise in RNA biology, custom data pipelines, and intuitive interface design, Riboscope helps research groups, biotech startups, and VC firms turn complex RNA data into clear insight and discovery.
