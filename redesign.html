About Riboscope

Founded in 2022, Riboscope is a specialist consultancy in RNA bioinformatics and scientific software development.

With deep expertise in RNA biology, custom data pipelines, and intuitive interface design, Riboscope helps research groups, biotech startups, and VC firms turn complex RNA data into clear insight and discovery.

Leadership

Riboscope is led by Anton Petrov, PhD, a bioinformatician with over 15 years of experience in RNA structure analysis, comparative genomics, and software development.

Anton previously led the Rfam and RNAcentral databases at EMBL-EBI, key resources used by tens of thousands of researchers worldwide. He is the creator of R2DT, a widely adopted platform for RNA secondary structure visualization.

He has authored over 30 peer-reviewed papers with 4,200+ citations, serves as a peer reviewer for leading journals including Nucleic Acids Research and Nature Communications, and has reviewed grants for international funding agencies.
